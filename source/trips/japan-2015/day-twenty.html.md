After my (by now) traditional breakfast of [Nattō](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natt%C5%8D) it was time to pack my bags and slowly make my way over to Haneda airport for my flight back. On my way there on the Yanamote Line I had to transfer at Shinagawa station, one of the few places I hadn't explored yet, so I stowed my bags in a train station locker and headed out.

The photo above on the left is pretty cool. [It shows a subterranean bycicle parking robot.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pcZSU40RBrg) You place your bike in front of the door and the machine takes your bike and places it below ground in a huge cilinder. A much nicer solution then the huge bycicle dumps around the stations in The Netherlands.

After a quick dinner it was time to take the last train in Japan to the airport and fly back to Europe.
