After landing at Newark Liberty International Airport from Amsterdam we took the bus to Allentown Pennsylvania to a relative where we stayed a few days to sleep off the Jetlag. After that we headed to the local airport to get our rental vehicle for the coming month, a Jeep Grand Cherokee, and headed west to Denver Colorado.
