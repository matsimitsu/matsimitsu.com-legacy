![DSC00487.jpg](https://d3khpbv2gxh34v.cloudfront.net/r/taiwan-2020/day-four/DSC00487.jpg "1.5")

![DSC00490.jpg](https://d3khpbv2gxh34v.cloudfront.net/r/taiwan-2020/day-four/DSC00490.jpg "1.5")
